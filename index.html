Tony Chopper

This little reindeer gained the ability to change its shape and think like humans after eating the Hito Hito no Mi fruit. Chopper is a valuable member of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, serving as the crew's doctor.

Roronoa Zoro

The first pirate (second member if we count Luffy) to join the Straw Hat Pirates crew, Zoro accepted Luffy's invitation after the captain saved his life.

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy, known as "Straw Hat Luffy," is the protagonist of the anime and the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates crew. From a very young age, his greatest dream has been to one day find the legendary treasure of Gol D. Roger and become the new Pirate King.
